Taxi Booking app is to provide a convenient way for users to book a taxi or ride-sharing service. The app should low users to easily locate and book a nearby driver , specify their pick-up and drop-off locations, and track driver's progress in real-time. The app should also provide users with information on the estimated fare d enable them to pay for the ride via the app itself. Additionally, the app may offer features such as driver ratings and reviews, ride history, and loyalty rewards to enhance the user experience. Ultimately, the purpose of a taxi booking app is to simplify the process of getting a ride, while also providing a safe and reliable means of transportation.

  • ✹ Use advanced GPS technologies like Assisted GPS to provide more accurate location information
  • ✹ Allow users to manually enter their pickup and drop-off locations as a backup option
  • ✹ Implement a secure and reliable payment gateway to ensure that transactions are completed successfully

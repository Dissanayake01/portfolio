Mayphil South Asia Distributor Project

Services:

UI/UX Design

Project Link:
https://mayphil.lk/
Client:

Mayphil South Asia Pvt Ltd (Perkins Distributor Sri Lanka)

Duration:

7 day

Mayphil South Asia is the only authorized distributor of Perkins engines and parts in Sri Lanka. They provide genuine products and after-sales services across industries like construction, agriculture, and power generation, backed by global standards and local expertise.

My Contribution I was the main UI/UX designer for this project, leading the full design process for their responsive website and landing page. From gathering requirements to delivering a clean, minimal, and conversion-friendly interface, I ensured the design aligned with Mayphil's brand identity and met their industrial audience’s needs.

  • ✹ Delivered the entire UI design within 15 days.
  • ✹ Met all client requirements around professionalism, clarity, and responsiveness.
  • ✹ Focused on creating a trustworthy and credible digital presence through design principles.

    • Design Thinking Process

    1. Empathize

  • ✹ Reviewed client requirements & industry audience expectations
  • ✹ Understood the challenges users face in locating reliable engine service providers

    • 2. Define

  • ✹ Hard to find official engine part suppliers online
  • ✹ Users need a quick way to validate trust and access contact info
  • ✹ Responsive site is key for mobile technicians and buyers on-site

    • 3. Ideate

  • ✹ Hero section with trust message and CTAs
  • ✹ Modular service section with categories
  • ✹ “Why Choose Us?” credibility block

    • 3. Prototype

  • ✹ Created high-fidelity UI in Figma
  • ✹ Implemented hierarchy, white space, clean grid layout
  • ✹ Blue-themed palette to match brand’s trust factor
  • ✹ Used modern Roboto typography for professionalism

    • 4. Test

  • ✹ Shared clickable prototype with client for feedback
  • ✹ Iterated based on visual tone, CTA clarity, and section spacing

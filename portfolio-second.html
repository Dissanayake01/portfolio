Overview : A Restaurant POS System is more than just payments - it’s about efficiency, organization, and seamless service. I designed a user-friendly POS interface that helps waiters manage orders, reservations, staff, and payments - all in one place.

Problem Statement: Managing a restaurant requires handling multiple tasks - orders, inventory, staff, reservations, and payments. Many POS systems are cluttered and difficult to navigate, causing inefficiencies in service.

Objective & Goals : Design a modern, intuitive POS system that enhances workflow for restaurant staff while offering powerful data insights for managers.

  • ✹ Implement surge pricing during peak hours to encourage more drivers to be available during busy periods
  • ✹ Use advanced GPS technologies like Assisted GPS to provide more accurate location information
  • ✹ Allow users to manually enter their pickup and drop-off locations as a backup option
  • ✹ Implement a secure and reliable payment gateway to ensure that transactions are completed successfully
  • ✹ Optimize the app's performance by minimizing load times and addressing technical issues promptly
